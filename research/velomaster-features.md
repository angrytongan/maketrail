# Velomaster obstacle catalog (Velosolutions)

Source: https://velosolutions.com/velomaster/

Prefab modular bike-skills features (wood/galvanised steel, built to order) — useful here as a catalog of **obstacle types worth supporting** beyond our current four (kicker/lander, berm, roller). The overview page lists no dimensions, angles, or spacing — those would be on individual product/spec pages, not fetched yet (same "log, don't read yet" treatment as [[sources-to-review]]).

## Feature types listed

Jump, Stairs, Hairpin, Small U-Turn, Seesaw, Snake, Rails, Camel, Small Hairpin, Corner, Sleeping Policeman, Sweeper (Left), Balance (beam), Bridge, Balance Game, Slalom Game.

## Candidates worth adding to our obstacle model

Of the 16, the ones that look like genuine geometric trail features (rather than playground games) and aren't already covered:
- **Seesaw** — pivoting plank, tips at a threshold weight/position. Now researched in depth, see [[teeter-totter]].
- **Rails / Balance (beam)** — narrow elevated beam, same concept as the "skinny" already logged in [[build-plans]]. Now researched in depth, see [[skinnies]].
- **Camel** — sounds like a double/triple roller hump (needs the product page to confirm — name isn't self-explanatory).
- **Sleeping Policeman** — literal speed bump, i.e. a single small roller.
- **Corner / Small U-Turn / Hairpin / Sweeper** — turn variants, likely just [[berms]] at different radii/angles rather than new obstacle types.

Also researched, not from this catalog but requested alongside the other two: **Drop** — see [[drops]].

## Material notes (real-world build, low priority for the simulator)

- Wood: pine/fir or "wooden polymer."
- Metal frames hot-dip galvanised.
- Stainless metric-thread fasteners; all wood edges rounded (safety detail, not geometry).

## Relevance to maketrail

- Confirms our obstacle list (kicker, lander, berm, roller) is a subset of a broader real-world taxonomy — **skinny/rail** and **seesaw** are the two clearest candidates for new obstacle types once the initial four are working.
- No numeric specs here to fold into [[jumps]] / [[berms]] / [[rollers]] yet — would need individual Velomaster product pages.
