# Pump track construction standards (Velosolutions)

Source: https://velosolutions.com/velosolutions-pump-track-standards/

General track-level standards, referenced by [[berms]] and [[rollers]] for the turn/roller-specific numbers. Kept separate because it's a distinct source and includes track-wide constants that don't belong in either file alone.

## Track geometry

- Minimum track width: 2m.
- Turn height must stay consistent from entrance to exit (see [[berms]] for the 1.30m minimum on >90° direction changes).
- Turn banking: minimum 55° standard, 50° absolute floor.
- Turn radius: minimum 3m, standard 4m.
- Roller spacing: minimum 3m; first roller adjacent to a turn must be ≥1m (standard 1.5m) from the turn's end.

## Materials (real-world build, not directly needed for the simulator but kept for completeness)

- Base: ≥200mm compacted crushed stone.
- Asphalt: laid at 100mm, compacted to 80mm; fine-aggregate-rich, high-binder, max 12mm aggregate, soft binder.

## Relevance to maketrail

- These are asphalt pump-track numbers — stricter/more compact than a natural dirt MTB trail. Treat as the "aggressive/pump-track" end of a difficulty spectrum, with [[berms]]'s beginner/intermediate/advanced bands as the natural-trail equivalent.
- Track width (2m minimum) is a reasonable default clearance/corridor width to leave either side of the marked trail line when generating the 3D trail surface.
